    The Tungsten Fabric Governance Working Group is one of three committees formed at the Tungsten Fabric Summit of June 2017. The GWG is tasked with evaluating options for foundations or other formal structures for structuring the Tungsten Fabric community in order to allow equal participation from contributors from multiple companies and to provide for shared maintenance of project infrastructure as well as marketing efforts to grow both the contributor and user communities.

    The GWG is likely a precursor to a Board of Directors or Board of Trustees. The actual Board structure will depend on the recommendations of the GWG.
