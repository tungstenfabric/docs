    • Core Committers¶

    The following are the core committers on all projects/repos in gerrit.tungsten.io.

    Core committers are the people who are able to give a +2/-2 review score in Gerrit and to merge patches into that repository.

    Docs¶

    https://gerrit.tungsten.io/r/admin/repos/docs
